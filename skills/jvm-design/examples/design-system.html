Design System · v Alpha

The
System.

A bold, editorial design system for Jung von Matt — assertive, precise, unmistakably German creative. Big letterforms, deep forest green, a single neon accent, and zero ornamentation.

Version · alpha Updated · 2026 Scope · Brand · Web · Slides

01 · Principles

Proof of conviction.

Heavy type

Carloschi Headline at scale. Always uppercase, always zero tracking. Letterforms do the talking.

Heavy color

Deep Forest Green is the canvas. Electric Neon Green is the pulse. White is the breath.

No compromise

Flat surfaces. Sharp edges. Zero ornament. Depth comes from contrast and scale, never effects.
